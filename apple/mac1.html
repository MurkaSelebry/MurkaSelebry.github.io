Apple Mac 1

Description

The Apple Mac 1, also known as the Apple I, was the first personal computer released by Apple Inc. in 1976. It was designed and hand-built by Steve Wozniak in Steve Jobs' garage, and sold for $666.66. The Mac 1 featured a 1 MHz MOS Technology 6502 processor, 4KB of memory, and a built-in video display. It did not come with a keyboard, case, or power supply, and users had to assemble and connect the components themselves.
